# Code of Conduct

The GDG Tech Learning Hub is committed to maintaining a respectful, inclusive, and professional environment for all contributors and learners.

This repository exists to promote structured learning and community growth.

---

## 🌍 Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming environment for everyone, regardless of:

- Experience level
- Background
- Identity
- Technical ability

Respect is non-negotiable.

---

## ✅ Expected Behavior

All participants and contributors are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Provide constructive feedback
- Support learners at all levels
- Encourage growth and collaboration
- Maintain integrity in contributions

---

## ❌ Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated:

- Harassment or discrimination
- Offensive or inappropriate language
- Plagiarism
- Spam or promotional content
- Disruptive or disrespectful communication

---

## ⚖ Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to:

- Remove inappropriate contributions
- Reject pull requests that violate standards
- Restrict participation if necessary

---

## 🤝 Community Responsibility

We are building a structured and long-term educational resource.

Every contribution should reflect professionalism, clarity, and respect.
