# Advanced Product Design

Advanced product design focuses on strategy and product thinking.

---

# Product Strategy

Designers must understand how design decisions affect business goals.

Examples include:

- product growth
- user retention
- market positioning

---

# Design Leadership

Senior designers help guide teams and influence product direction.

Responsibilities include:

- mentoring designers
- guiding design decisions
- collaborating with engineers and product managers

---

# Continuous Learning

Product design evolves constantly.

Stay updated by studying:

- new design trends
- successful products
- emerging technologies
