# Product Design (UI/UX)

Product design focuses on creating digital products that are useful, usable, and enjoyable for users.

Product designers combine:

- user research
- interface design
- usability testing
- product thinking

to build products that solve real problems.

Product design is used in:

- mobile applications
- websites
- software platforms
- digital services
- SaaS products

---

# Who This Track Is For

This track is suitable for:

- beginners interested in UI/UX design
- developers who want to understand design
- creatives interested in digital product design
- anyone curious about how digital products are designed

# What You Will Learn

By following this track you will learn how to:

- understand user needs
- design user interfaces

# Learning Approach

Product design improves through:

- observing real products
- practicing interface design
- analyzing user behavior
- iterating on designs

Design is a **continuous learning process**.
