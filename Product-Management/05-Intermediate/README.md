# Intermediate Product Management

This section explores deeper product management concepts used in real organizations.

---

# Product Strategy

Product strategy defines the long-term direction of a product.

It answers questions such as:

- What problem are we solving?
- Who are our users?
- How will we compete in the market?

---

# Stakeholder Management

Product managers collaborate with:

- engineers
- designers
- marketers
- executives

Strong communication skills are essential.

---

# Product Analytics

Product managers use data to evaluate product performance.

Tools include:

- Google Analytics
- Mixpanel
- Amplitude
