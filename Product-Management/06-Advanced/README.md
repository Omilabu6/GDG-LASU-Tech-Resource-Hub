# Advanced Product Management

Advanced product management focuses on leading product teams and scaling successful products.

---

# Product Leadership

Senior product managers guide product direction and mentor teams.

Responsibilities include:

- defining product vision
- aligning teams
- making strategic decisions

---

# Scaling Products

Successful products must scale to support more users.

This includes:

- improving infrastructure
- optimizing performance
- expanding product features

---

# Continuous Learning

Product management evolves with technology and markets.

Successful product managers continuously learn from:

- user feedback
- product analytics
- industry trends
