# Product Management

Product management focuses on identifying problems, building solutions, and delivering products that create value for users and businesses.

Product managers work at the intersection of:

- technology
- design
- business
- users

They guide the development of products from idea to launch and continuously improve them based on feedback and data.

Product management is used in industries such as:

- software development
- mobile applications
- startups
- enterprise products
- SaaS platforms
