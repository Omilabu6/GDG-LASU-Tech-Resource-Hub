# Product Management Fundamentals

This section introduces the core ideas behind product management.

---

# 1. What is Product Management?

Product management is the process of guiding a product from idea to launch and ensuring it solves real user problems.

Product managers focus on:

- identifying user needs
- defining product features
- prioritizing development
- measuring product success

# 2. Understanding Users

Successful products start with understanding users.

Common methods include:

- user interviews
- surveys
- observing user behavior
- analyzing feedback

Understanding user needs helps product managers build better solutions.

---

# 3. The Product Lifecycle

Products typically move through several stages:

1. Idea
2. Development
3. Launch
4. Growth
5. Improvement

Product managers help guide the product through each stage.

---

# 4. Product vs Project Management

Product Management focuses on building the **right product**.

Project Management focuses on delivering the **project on time**.

Both roles collaborate but have different responsibilities.
